About Me

Each project featured below reflects my commitment to creating intuitive, accessible, and user-centered digital experiences. From nonprofit websites to e-commerce platforms and enterprise dashboards, I’ve led UX initiatives that blend research, strategy, and design to drive measurable results. My approach is grounded in collaboration, data-informed decisions, and a deep understanding of both user needs and business goals.

I'm Orlando Ruiz, a Chicago-based Web Developer and CMS Specialist with over a decade of experience building high-impact, user-centric digital experiences. My background spans a wide range of industries, where I've delivered responsive websites, email campaigns, and e-commerce solutions that drive engagement and conversions. With expertise in tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and CMS platforms such as AEM, WordPress, and Sitecore, I bring both creative vision and technical precision to every project.

Whether optimizing content for SEO, designing intuitive user interfaces, or managing large-scale CMS migrations, I thrive at the intersection of design, development, and strategy. My collaborative approach and strong communication skills have helped cross-functional teams turn complex ideas into clean, accessible digital products. I'm passionate about using design and code to solve real-world problems, and I’m always looking for new ways to improve user experiences and deliver measurable results.
